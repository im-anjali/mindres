The game of Innovations - where Tech grows Expo(nentially) is specifically curated to bring forth the best talents in the ever tech evolving world, inviting dedicated tech enthusiasts to participate in a theme-based competition. Participants in this event will have the opportunity to showcase their innovative projects at TechExpo, MindSpark'23 with each entry being carefully evaluated for its creativity and uniqueness. This event will provide a platform for the young tech enthusiasts to showcase their products to be viewed by college students, school students, and industry professionals alike.

Structure

Round 1:

Participants will be shortlisted based on the innovation and creativity demonstrated in their projects. To participate in this round, teams are required to submit detailed project ideas, including project description, designs, recognitions, photos, and video links in a pdf format.

Round 2 (TechExpo Stall: 3 Days):

The top contestants from Round 1 will be given the opportunity to showcase their projects at TechExpo, MindSpark'23 on 27th, 28th, 29th October 2023.

Deadline

Round-1: 22nd October 2023

Winning Criteria:

The winning project will be determined through a voting system, and a cash prize of ₹ 5000 will be awarded to the victors.
